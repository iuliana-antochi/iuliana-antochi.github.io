About me

Hi! I'm Iuliana and I'm working on becoming a front-end developer. Once I realized my previous job doesn't suit me and didn't bring joy, I've started to look up on "How to become a front-end developer?"

The internet has unlimited resources, but I've chosen to start by enrolling in the Bootcamp available on Frontend Masters website. This Bootcamp taught me the basics of HTML, CSS and JavaScript and after finishing it, I felt like I want to learn more.

So, I've joined the Front-End Engineer Career Path from Codecademy. It's a hard, but fun journey where I've learned a lot of interesting things including making this portfolio you're reading right now.

In my free time I love to play with my cat, Dora, and cooking new recipes or baking delicious cakes. Another passion would be photography, but this requires a lot of time and travel, and I'd also love to learn to crochet and knit.
